My Projects
Brain Vessels are tortuous structures and it is very difficult to use traditional techniques for semantic segmentation in brain images. In this project we explore several techniques that aim at segmenting small objects in large datasets. We used PyTorch, Tensorflow and Keras for different stages of our development process and used Colab for development environment.
