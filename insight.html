Insight@UCD Festival

AI in the field of medicine espically medical images is becoming increasingly popular. Generative methods are being used to not only synthesise data but also for object detection and segmentation. The first game "Real or AI" is designed to engage the player into asking whether a painting is made by a real artist or generated by AI. The aim is to show the power of generative methods and their ability to mimic real art styles.

The second game "Spot the Difference" engages the viewer to find differences in two images. This is the basic idea of my research in identifying new or enhancing multiple sclerosis lesions. Finding new lesions is difficult and prone to bias of the observer. The game puts the user in a time crunch and has to race against time to spot the differences.
